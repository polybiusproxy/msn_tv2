If your is connected to your home network, you can use it to view photos that are stored on your computer. To view photos that are on your computer, you must make sure your is connected to your home network, and that your computer is set up to share files over the network.

Introduction to home networking

You don't have to sign in to the to view the photos that are stored on your computer. Just turn on your and follow these steps:

  1. Make sure your and your computer are connected to your home network, and that the photos folder on the computer is a shared folder.
  2. Choose Photos from the bottom of the Sign-in page.

    3. The Photos Home page appears.

  3. In the Photos on home network section, choose the computer folder that contains the photos you want to view.

    4. Small images representing the photos appear on screen. (If there are subfolders within a photos folder on the computer, you'll need to choose a subfolder to see the photos it contains.)

Check the boxes next to the photos you want to work with. Then you can do the following:
  • Choose View Slideshow to see a slideshow of the slected photos, with each photo on screen for a few seconds.
  • Choose Print Photos to print copies of the selected photos. (You must have a compatible USB printer attached to your . For information about printing, sign in and choose Help and then Printing.)
  • Choose Send Photos to include the selected photos in an e-mail message. (You must sign in to the to send the e-mail.
  • Choose Save Photos to save copies of the selected photos in albums on your . (For more information on creating photo albums, choose Photos from the bottom of the Sign-in page, and then choose Help from the Photos Home page.)
