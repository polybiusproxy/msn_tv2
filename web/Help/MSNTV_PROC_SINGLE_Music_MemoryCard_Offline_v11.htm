With MSN TV Music, you can use your to play music tracks that are stored on a memory card. To listen to the tracks, you must first attach a compatible USB memory card reader (sold separately) to your , and then insert the card that contains the tracks.

To learn more about attaching a memory card reader, sign in to the and choose Help and then Music.

You don't have to sign in to the to listen to the music tracks that are stored on a memory card. Just turn on your and follow these steps:
  1. Attach a compatible memory card reader to one of the USB ports on the back of your .
  2. Insert a card that contains music tracks into the card reader.

    3. A message tells you that you've inserted a USB storage device.

  3. Choose Music.

    4. A playlist of Music Tracks appears on screen. (If any of the files on the memory card are in folders, the folder names are listed as well. Choose a folder to open it.)

  4. Check the box next to each track you want to work with. Then you can do the following:
  • Choose Play to play the selected track(s).
  • Choose Add Selections to save copies of the selected tracks in a playlist on your . (Choose Music from the Sign-in page, and go to My selections to see your playlist.
