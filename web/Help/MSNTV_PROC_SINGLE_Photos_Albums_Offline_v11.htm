You can store up to 100 photos on your , and arrange them in albums. Once you've placed your photos in an album, you can view them on your TV as a slide show or a screen saver.

Important: Photos are saved on your at a resolution that's suitable for viewing on a TV. If you want to keep high-resolution versions of your photos, make sure to store the originals elsewhere.

You don't have to sign in to the to save photos in albums. Just turn on your and choose Photos from the Sign-in page.

To save photos in an album, you must first select the photos from a memory card or from a shared folder on a computer that's connected to your home network.
