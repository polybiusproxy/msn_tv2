If your is connected to your home network, you can use it to watch videos that are stored on your computer.

Introduction to home networking

You don't have to sign in to the to watch videos that are stored on your computer. Just turn on your and follow these steps:

  1. Make sure your and your computer are connected to your home network, and that the videos folder on the computer is a shared folder.
  2. Choose Videos from the bottom of the Sign-in page.

    3. The Videos page appears.

  3. In the Videos on home network section, choose the computer folder that contains the videos you want to watch.

    4. A list of videos appears on screen. (If any of the files on the memory card are in folders, the folder names are listed as well. Choose a folder to open it.)

  4. Check the box next to each video you want to play, and then choose Play.
