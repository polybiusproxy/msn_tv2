With MSN TV Videos, you can use your to play videos that are stored on a memory card. To watch the videos, you must first attach a compatible USB memory card reader (sold separately) to your , and then insert the card that contains the clips.

To learn more about attaching a memory card reader, sign in to the and choose Help and then Videos.

You don't have to sign in to the to watch the videos that are stored on a memory card. Just turn on your and follow these steps:
  1. Attach a compatible memory card reader to one of the USB ports on the back of your .
  2. Insert a card that contains videos into the card reader.

    3. A message tells you that you've inserted a USB storage device.

  3. Choose Videos.

    4. A list of videos appears on screen. (If any of the files on the memory card are in folders, the folder names are listed as well. Choose a folder to open it.)

  4. Check the box next to each video you want to work with. Then you can choose Play to play the selected video(s).
    5. For more information about using Videos, sign in to the and choose Help and then Videos.
