With MSN TV Photos, you can view photos you take with your digital camera. To view your photos, you must first attach a compatible USB memory card reader (sold separately) to your , and then insert the card that contains the photos.

To learn more about attaching a memory card reader, sign in to the and choose Help and then Photos.

You don't have to sign in to the to view the photos that are stored on a memory card. Just turn on your and follow these steps:
  1. Attach a compatible memory card reader to one of the USB ports on the back of your .
  2. Insert a card that contains photos into the card reader.

    3. A message tells you that you've inserted a USB storage device.

  3. Choose Photos.

    4. Small images representing the photos on the card appear on screen.

  4. Check the boxes next to the photos you want to work with. Then you can do the following:
  • Choose View Slideshow to display each selected photo for a few seconds.
  • Choose Send Photos to include the selected photos in an e-mail message. (You must sign in to the to send the e-mail.
  • Choose Print Photos to print copies of the selected photos. (You must have a compatible USB printer attached to your . (For information about printing, sign in and choose Help and then Printing.)
  • Choose Save Photos to save copies of the selected photos in albums on your . (For more information on creating photo albums, choose Photos from the bottom of the Sign-in page, and then choose Help from the Photos Home page.)
  • Choose Delete Photos to remove the selected photos from the memory card. (WARNING: Choosing Delete Photos permanently removes the selected photos from the memory card and from your .)
