You can store music tracks on your and arrange them in playlists. Arrange your playlists by music genre, artist, song title, or any method you like.

You don't have to sign in to the to create your own music playlists. Just turn on your and choose Music from the Sign-in page.

To save music tracks in a playlist, you must first select the tracks from a memory card or from a shared folder on a computer that's connected to your home network.

To save copies of music tracks on your , follow these steps:

  1. Connect your to a home network with shared music folders, or attach a memory card reader and insert a card that contains music files.
  2. Check the boxes next to the tracks you want to put in a playlist, choose Add Selections.

    3. The selected tracks are added to a playlist.

  3. To see the playlist, choose Music from the Sign-in page, and then choose the tracks in the My selections section of the Music page,
  4. Check the box next to each track you want to play, and then choose Play.

    5. For detailed instructions on using playlists, sign in to the and choose Help and then Music.
