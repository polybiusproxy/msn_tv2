MSN TV Music lets you listen to music on your . You can listen to music tracks that are stored on a memory card, or save the tracks in a playlist on your and listen to them at any time. If your is connected to your home network, you can listen to music tracks that are in shared folders on your home computer.

Some Music features require additional equipment that's sold separately:

  • To listen to music tracks that are stored on a memory card, you'll need a compatible USB memory card reader.
  • To listen to music tracks that are on your home computer, you'll need to connect your to a home network using a hub, router, or wireless connection.
You can use many Music features without signing in to the . Simply turn on your and choose Music from the Sign-in page to:

  • Listen to music tracks that are on a memory card
  • Listen to music tracks that are on your home computer (if you've connected your to a home network and set up file sharing on your computer)
  • Copy music tracks from a memory card or a shared folder on your home computer to your , and create your own music playlist
To listen to a track, follow these steps:
  1. Choose the checkbox beside a track in a playlist. (You can check several tracks at once.)
  2. Choose the Play button.

    3. The track(s) will open and play in Windows Media Player. (If the track does not start playing, choose Media Player's Play button.)

Note: Media Player supports the follow audio formats: AIFF, AU, MP3, WAV, and WMA.

For more information about playing music tracks, sign in to the and choose Help and then Music.
