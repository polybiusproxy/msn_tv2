If you've purchased music from an online source (MSN Music, for example) and downloaded it to your home computer, you can play that music using your Internet & Media Player.

Some music files use Windows Media digital rights management (DRM) for file protection. To play DRM-protected files that are on a computer on your home network, you must first download, install, and run a free application called Windows Media Connect on your computer.

To download Windows Media Connect, use your computer's browser to go to www.microsoft.com, and then search for Windows Media Connect and follow the instructions you see.

On your computer, use the Windows Media Connect Wizard to select the folders that contain your music files, and then select the Internet & Media Player as a device that can share those folders.
