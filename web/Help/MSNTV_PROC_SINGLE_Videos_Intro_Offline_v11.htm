MSN TV Videos lets you watch videos on your . You can watch videos that are stored on a memory card, or, if your is connected to your home network, you can watch videos that are in shared folders on your home computer.

Some Videos features require additional equipment that's sold separately:

  • To watch videos that are stored on a memory card, you'll need a compatible USB memory card reader.
  • To watch videos that are on your home computer, you'll need to connect your to a home network using a hub, router, or wireless connection.
You can use many Videos features without signing in to the . Simply turn on your and choose Videos from the Sign-in page to:

  • Watch videos that are on a memory card
  • Watch videos that are on your home computer (if you've connected your to a home network and set up file sharing on your computer)
To watch a video, choose the checkbox next to it, and then choose Play. It will open and play in Windows Media Player.

Media Player supports the following video formats: ASF, MPEG, and WMV.

For more information about using Videos, sign in to the and choose Help and then Videos.
