Introduction to home networking

You don't have to sign in to the to listen to the music tracks that are stored on your computer. Just turn on your and follow these steps:

  1. Make sure your and your computer are connected to your home network, and that the music folder on the computer is a shared folder.
  2. Choose Music from the bottom of the Sign-in page.

    3. The Music page appears.

  3. In the Music on home network section, choose the computer folder that contains the music you want to hear.

    4. A list of music tracks appears on screen. (If there are subfolders within a music folder on the computer, you'll need to choose a subfolder to see the clips it contains.)

Check the boxes next to the tracks you want to work with. Then you can do the following:
  • Choose Play to play the selected track(s).
  • Choose Add Selections to save copies of the selected tracks in a playlist on your . (Choose Music from the Sign-in page, and go to My selections to see your playlist.
