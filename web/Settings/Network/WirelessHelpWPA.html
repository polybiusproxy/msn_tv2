You have reached the Enter wireless network pass phrase page because your wireless router or access point requires a special WPA pass phrase in order to connect. A pass phrase is between 8 and 63 characters which can include letters, numbers, spaces and punctuation and is case sensitive.

Enter the pass phrase exactly as it appears on the WPA setup page of your router or access point. (You might find it useful to print your router’s setup page using a computer on your home network.)
