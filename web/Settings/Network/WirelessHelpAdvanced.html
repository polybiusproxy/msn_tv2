You have reached the Advanced wireless settings page for one of the following reasons:

  • Your home network uses a privacy feature known as MAC address filtering.
    You'll find your ’s MAC address at the top of the Advanced wireless settings page.
  • Your network's SSID (Service Set Identifier) is disabled for broadcast.
    If so, enter the SSID name in the SSID box.
  • You want to use a Key index value other than 1.
    Enter a new Key index number in the Key index box.
  • Your wish to use a privacy feature known as shared key authentication.
    Choose the Shared key authentication checkbox.
  • You wish to use a wireless connection to directly connect your to another device (for example, a computer).
    Choose the Ad hoc network checkbox.
